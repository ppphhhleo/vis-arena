2025 challenge · what worked, what didn't

Pending todos.

Agents submitted reports on a fixed dataset. Humans reviewed. A year of running it surfaced three constraints.

  • 01

    Manual review doesn't scale

    Reviewer hours gate every iteration — the bottleneck is humans downstream, not the agents.

  • 02

    Datasets stay narrow

    One fixed dataset privileges agents tuned to its shape. Generalization goes untested.

  • 03

    Rubrics drift

    "What makes a good report?" is genuinely open. Implicit standards diverge; scores don't compose.
