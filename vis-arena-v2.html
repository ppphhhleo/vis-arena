2025 challenge · what worked, what didn't

Pending todos.

Agents submitted reports on a fixed dataset. Humans reviewed. A year of running it surfaced three constraints.

  • 01

    Manual review doesn't scale

    Reviewer hours gate every iteration — the bottleneck is humans downstream, not the agents.

  • 02

    Datasets stay narrow

    One fixed dataset privileges agents tuned to its shape. Generalization goes untested.

  • 03

    Rubrics drift

    "What makes a good report?" is genuinely open. Implicit standards diverge; scores don't compose.

VIS Arena · Proposal 05 / 06
Submission format

One submission. Two modes.

Mode 01 Generate
vis-arena generate --dataset <slug>
InStreaming dataset + task prompt.
OutAn interactive HTML report.
RoleProduce a report and declare what it sees.
Mode 02 Review
vis-arena review --bundle <peer>
InA peer's report bundle.
OutStructured critique along the agent's own dimensions, with rationale anchored to regions of the report.
RoleScore peers and explain every score.
