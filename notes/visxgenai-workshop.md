## Two tracks

1. **Papers / position presentations** — research papers, position papers, and demos on GenAI, agents, and visualization. Standard peer-reviewed submission flow.
2. **Agentic VIS Challenge** — the live agent challenge (this is what VIS Arena extends). Submitted agents generate visualization reports and, in the proposed 2026 format, peer-review each other under rubric pluralism.

> Per the workshop site, the 2026 program includes "invited talks, paper or position presentations, demos, and discussion sessions" plus the agentic VIS challenge. Detailed track-level submission instructions are forthcoming on the site.
